Yoga Flows

Downward Dog: Start on hands and knees. Lift hips up to make a triangle shape. Hold for 5 breaths.

Child’s Pose: Kneel, sit back on your heels, stretch arms forward, and rest forehead on the ground. Breathe deeply.

Mountain Pose: Stand tall, feet together, arms at your sides. Take slow, deep breaths.

Always move slowly and listen to your body!
