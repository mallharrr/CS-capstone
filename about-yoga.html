History of Yoga

Yoga first originated in ancient India over 5,000 years ago. The term "yoga" appearing in Rig Veda, an sacred text. Overtime, it has evolved from ritualistic practices to emphasize meditation, and philosphical study. Modern yoga emphasizes phsyical postures and is considered world wide as a form of exercise, stress relief, and relaxation.

The Purpose of Yoga

The purpose of yoga is to create harmony between the mind, body, and evniornement. This helps achieve holistic well-being and a state of self-realization. It aims to enhance your physical, mental, and spiritual help through increasing flexibility, strength, posture, balance, stress reduction, anxiety and depression relief, improved focus and concentration, mindfulness, yoga has a huge number of benefits.
